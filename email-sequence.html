Email Sequence - Ricky Gutierrez / Learn Plan Profit

Built: 2026-05-16 Scope: The owned-email cadence underneath the four-piece install. Three sequences: (A) the cold-prospect scorecard nurture, (B) the post-cohort-application sequence, (C) the daily-live recap and the post-buy LPP onboarding. All copy stays operator-grounded, niche-bounded, and investment-niche compliant.

Final copy locks with Ricky on day one of the install. This document is the framework and the v1 templates.

Sequence A. Scorecard Nurture (cold prospect, by score band)

Trigger: scorecard completion + email captured.

The sequence forks based on the score band returned. Three sub-sequences run in parallel - one per band.

A1. The "Too Early" sub-sequence (score 0-39)

This is the audience that should not buy LPP yet. The job of this sequence is to keep them in the daily-live + YouTube content loop until they are ready, without ever pushing them to the $699 buy.

Segmentation tags applied across all sequences

  • score_band (early / lpp_ready / cohort_ready)
  • capital_band (under_1k / 1_5k / 5_25k / 25_100k / 100k_plus)
  • language (en / es / both)
  • daily_live_subscriber (true / false)
  • lpp_graduate (true / false)
  • cohort_applicant (true / false)
  • cohort_enrolled (true / false)

Every email surface uses these tags to route the conditional CTAs. The recap email is the single template that adapts across all tag bands.

What this sequence does not do

  • No subject line uses "make $X," "trade like me," "guaranteed," or any return-claim language
  • No body copy quotes a P&L number
  • No CTA promises a return rate
  • No sequence ever sends to a tag band the scorecard routed away from the buy (a "Too Early" recipient never gets the LPP buy email)
  • No sequence ever forwards the daily watchlist as a "trade these" recommendation - the watchlist is observation, not recommendation
  • The cohort sequence never lists a dollar price in the email body - the application surface holds the price
