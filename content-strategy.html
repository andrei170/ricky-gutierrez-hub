The content engine is built. The capture engine underneath it is not.

What changes after the four-piece install

Every piece of existing content gets a single new asset routing CTA: the scorecard. Not the LPP buy link. Not the cohort link. The scorecard. The scorecard handles the downstream routing.

This is the cleanest behaviour change in the content layer:

  • YouTube long-form upload description: "Take the 90-second readiness quiz at [scorecard URL]"
  • IG bio: scorecard URL replaces the Teachable direct link
  • TikTok bio: scorecard URL
  • Daily live YouTube pinned comment: scorecard URL + watchlist opt-in URL
  • Daily live YouTube end-card: scorecard URL
  • LPP Group Chat (paid alumni only): the cohort application URL (not the scorecard - graduates skip the scorecard and route directly to the cohort filter)

The scorecard absorbs the cold traffic. The cohort application absorbs the warm alumni traffic. The LPP buy stays as the explicit retail purchase for the audience segment that scores into the LPP-ready band.

Content pillars (proposed - lock with Ricky day one)

These are the four content pillars that already exist in Ricky's content history, surfaced explicitly so the scorecard and cohort messaging maps cleanly to what the audience already expects:

Pillar one. The daily-live transparency

What Ricky shows on the 9:30 AM EST live - the watchlist, the plan, the trades, the discipline log. This is the existing transparency engine. The post-live recap email cadence (from Piece Four of the install) extends this pillar into owned email.

Pillar two. Beginner-first instruction

The "Learn Plan Profit" three-step frame. The YouTube long-form catalogue that explains nasdaq ETFs, blue-chip stock mechanics, and risk discipline at the beginner level. The scorecard's "Too Early" route sends the early-stage audience back into this pillar.

Pillar three. Bilingual reach

Spanish CCs across LPP, Spanish-language reels and TikToks. The /lista watchlist opt-in is the dedicated Spanish-language capture surface. The Spanish VSL variant is the dedicated Spanish-language buy surface. A long-tail expansion of this pillar is the LATAM-specific reel series.

Pillar four. The operator-scale lane (NEW - the cohort tier)

This pillar does not yet exist publicly. The cohort install creates it. Content recommendations: - One YouTube long-form per quarter walking what the LPP Operator Cohort actually looks like (the 2-day in-person, the 90-day runway, the discipline curriculum) - One IG reel per month featuring a cohort graduate (after the first cohort completes - placeholder for the inaugural cycle) - The cohort landing page as the static reference for the pillar

Content cadence (no new production load on Ricky)

The install does not require Ricky to produce additional content. The cadence that produced 1.2M YouTube subscribers continues unchanged. The install changes what the existing content routes to.

The two new pieces Ricky records once: - The English VSL (5-7 minutes, one record day at the Techbuds Trading HQ) - The Spanish VSL (5-7 minutes, same record day)

After Day 7, no new content is required from Ricky for the funnel to operate. The scorecard runs. The cohort application runs. The daily-live recap email runs off Ricky's existing live commentary (the recap is a 3-bullet summary written by Ricky's hand or by the AG1 team off the live transcript - locks day one).

What AG1 does not produce

  • Trading content. Ricky produces all trading content. AG1 does not opine on nasdaq ETFs or blue-chip stocks.
  • The watchlist. Ricky writes the watchlist for the daily-live recap email. AG1 builds the email surface.
  • The cohort curriculum. Ricky teaches the cohort. AG1 builds the application, the landing, and the post-cycle alumni surface.
  • The VSL voice. Ricky records the VSL. AG1 writes the script in collaboration and edits the cut.

What AG1 produces (content-adjacent infrastructure only)

  • The scorecard questions, scoring logic, routing rules, bottleneck readout templates
  • The VSL script (English + Spanish) - co-written with Ricky day one
  • The cohort landing page copy (locked with Ricky day one)
  • The post-application email sequence (3-email cohort onboarding)
  • The daily-live recap email template (Ricky fills the watchlist content; AG1 owns the cadence and the segmentation)
  • The /watchlist and /lista opt-in landings (mirror, bilingual)
  • The retargeting pixel and segmentation logic
  • The cohort group communication surface (Discord channel or Skool space - locks day one)

Cross-language content note

The Spanish CC infrastructure inside LPP is the unmonetised asset. The four-piece install respects it explicitly: - Scorecard captures language preference at Q7 and routes accordingly - VSL has a Spanish variant filmed the same day as the English variant - /lista is the Spanish equivalent of /watchlist - same email cadence, Spanish copy - Cohort welcomes bilingual seats and the application surfaces the language preference

No Spanish content is produced separately for the install. The existing Spanish-language reels and TikToks continue. The install adds Spanish-language capture infrastructure under the existing content.
