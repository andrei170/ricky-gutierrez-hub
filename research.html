Top Funnel Gaps (publicly observable)

One. No VSL on learnplanprofit.net or the Teachable LPP 3.0 page.

1.2M YouTube subscribers and 294K IG followers land on a static text-and-image page, then on a Teachable card with a "Sign Up" button. The bridge from the YouTube voice to the buy decision is missing. Andrew Aziz (Bear Bull Traders), Mark Minervini, and Cameron Fous all run a video pre-frame between cold organic traffic and the paid offer. Ricky's audience is multiples of theirs and the asset is missing.

Two. No qualification or readiness step before checkout.

Anyone with $699 and a working card can buy. There is no scorecard, quiz, or application gate. This shows up in the public review ecosystem as "the course is light on real strategy, heavy on basics" - the recurring criticism in third-party LPP reviews is fit mismatch (people buying who are not at the right stage of trading readiness). A pre-purchase readiness scorecard is the cleanest fix for that mismatch and the cleanest way to lift cohort conversion downstream.

Three. No tier between $699 LPP and the implied higher-touch lane.

The Techbuds Trading HQ is a physical facility. Daily live trading is broadcast from it. The 1,000+ LPP Group Chat alumni are the warmest possible list for a mid-tier above $699 - a cohort or mentorship priced in the mid-four-figure to low-five-figure range. Peer-category operators all run this slot: Tim Sykes Millionaire Challenge ($3-5K application-gated), Ross Cameron Inner Circle ($9,997 application-only), Mark Minervini Master Trader ($4-5K plus $6K/yr Private Access), Cameron Fous FOUS4 ($1,997). Ricky's audience size supports the slot above $9,997 when the cohort is application-gated, capped, and respects the beginner-first brand.

Four. No email capture for daily live trading session attendees.

The 9:30 AM EST live is the deepest transparency hook in the category. It is also the largest unmonetised retention surface in the funnel. There is no pre-live email opt-in ("get tomorrow's watchlist before the open"), no post-live email capture ("get the recap, the trade log, and tomorrow's setups"), and no retargeting list built off the live URL. Every attendee who shows up, watches, and leaves is invisible to nurture.

Niche Guardrails (Trading-Education)

  • Day-trading education space is under active SEC and FTC scrutiny. Warrior Trading reached an FTC settlement in 2022 over earnings claims. All cohort copy, outreach copy, and landing copy avoid specific income claims, P&L promises, or "make $X with me" framing.
  • Cohort positioning sits at the level of risk discipline, plan adherence, and operator development - not "make money fast."
  • Bilingual angle is genuine, not invented. LPP already ships with Spanish CCs. The Spanish-language TOFU funnel is the real unmonetised asset.
  • Beginner-first brand respect. The cohort is positioned as "post-LPP, ready-to-scale" - not "for advanced traders only" - so it does not betray the brand voice.

Risk Notes

  • Regulatory tone caution. Investment-niche copy stays method-grounded. No income claims on the landing page. No "make $X" copy anywhere in the package. Footer disclaimer mandatory on every public-surface deliverable.
  • No fabricated tech claims. Every gap listed is observable from the public surfaces named above. No assumptions about backend infrastructure.
  • Beginner-first brand respect. Cohort copy positions the mid-tier as the natural next step for the LPP graduate who is ready to scale account size with risk discipline. Not as an "advanced-only" tier.
  • Mixed-review reality. The recurring third-party criticism is fit mismatch, not content quality. The scorecard fix addresses that mismatch directly - it filters at the gate, not after the buy.
