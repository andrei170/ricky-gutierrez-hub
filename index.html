The Four Pieces

Four installs. Sit on top of the existing funnel.

No course rebuild. No re-platform. No brand pivot. The four pieces patch the gaps between the YouTube voice, the buy decision, and the warm alumni list.

Piece 01 · Highest-Leverage Pre-Sell
01

The 5-7 minute VSL on learnplanprofit.net + the Teachable storefront.

The buy decision currently happens on a static text-and-image page. No video. No analyst voice. No three-step Learn Plan Profit walk before the Sign Up button. 1.2M YouTube subscribers and 294K IG followers convert (or do not) on bullet points. The fix is a 5-7 minute VSL filmed at the Techbuds Trading HQ, embedded on the homepage and above the Teachable curriculum bullets. Spanish variant filmed the same day.

We build the 5-7 minute VSL that pre-sells LPP for the right buyer. Script, direct the record, edit the cut, embed on both surfaces, set captions. The voice on screen is Ricky's.

Compounds against every YouTube CTA, every IG link-in-bio click, and every Spanish-language entry point.

Piece 02 · The Qualification Step
02

The 90-second readiness scorecard between the audience and the buy.

The recurring third-party criticism of LPP is fit mismatch: "good for beginners, less useful once you are already trading," "no clear next step." That is not a content problem. Anyone with a card can buy regardless of where they are on the readiness curve. The fix is a 10-question quiz that returns Too Early, Ready For LPP, or Ready For The Cohort plus a 3-line bottleneck readout. Captures email. Tags by capital, language, and prior trading experience. The scorecard is the upstream router.

We install the "Are You Ready For Day Trading?" scorecard that filters before checkout. Question logic, scoring algorithm, routing rules, bottleneck readouts, email tagging. The recommendations route through Ricky's three-step frame.

Filters fit-mismatched buyers out of LPP, lifts review quality downstream, captures email at scale for the first time.

Piece 03 · The Mid-Tier Mentorship
03

The LPP Operator Cohort - the unfilled slot between the course and the higher-touch lane.

The 1,000+ LPP Group Chat alumni are the warmest possible list for the tier above the course and currently have no upgrade path. Every peer in the category fills this slot. The Techbuds Trading HQ is the moat. No other operator at this audience tier has a physical in-person facility already running. Format: 6-8 verified day traders, application-gated via Typeform, mid-tier mentorship pricing, 2-day in-person at the Arizona HQ plus a 90-day implementation runway. Bilingual seats welcome.

We build the application and landing for the LPP Operator Cohort. Cohort landing copy, Typeform application, scorecard-to-cohort routing, post-application email sequence, alumni surface. Ricky teaches the cohort.

Respects the beginner-first brand. Positioned as the natural next step for the LPP graduate ready to scale account size with risk discipline.

Piece 04 · The Daily-Live Capture
04

The 9:30 AM EST live is the largest unmonetised retention surface in the funnel.

Five mornings a week, the deepest transparency hook in the day-trading-education category fires into the void. No pre-live opt-in. No post-live recap email. No retargeting pixel built off the live URL. Every attendee who shows up, watches, and leaves is invisible to nurture. The fix is a two-part install: a /watchlist landing capturing email pre-live, plus a daily 3-bullet recap email sent post-live. Bilingual /lista variant for the Spanish-speaking slice.

We write the email sequence that retains the live-session attendees. Pre-live watchlist opt-in, daily recap template, segmentation tags, retargeting pixel wiring, English and Spanish variants. The watchlist content is Ricky's.

Builds the warmest possible audience for any future paid-media spend without changing the daily-live cadence.
