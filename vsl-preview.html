What this VSL does not do

  • It does not show a P&L screen
  • It does not name a return number or a "made $X" claim
  • It does not promise a trading outcome
  • It does not show a live trade execution
  • It does not use options, crypto, or penny stocks vocabulary
  • It does not show the price of LPP (the $699 price stays on Ricky's checkout, not in the VSL)
  • It does not name the cohort price (the application surfaces the cohort cycle's pricing)
