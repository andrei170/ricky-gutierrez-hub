The four-piece install, sequenced

Piece One. The Pre-Sell VSL

Surface: Embedded on learnplanprofit.net (hero) and on the Teachable LPP 3.0 storefront (above the curriculum bullets).

Length: 5-7 minutes English variant, 5-7 minutes Spanish variant (Ricky speaks both, the bilingual asset is leveraged).

Script structure (in Ricky's voice): 1. Open (0:00-0:45) - the daily-live opener format the audience already trusts. "I trade live every morning at 9:30 AM EST. Here is why what I show on YouTube is not enough on its own." 2. Learn / Plan / Profit thesis (0:45-2:00) - the three-step frame in Ricky's voice. The course exists because beginners need a structured Learn step, a structured Plan step, and a Profit step that is actually about plan adherence, not P&L promises. 3. Three LPP graduate stories (2:00-4:30) - one beginner who finally built a Plan they actually followed, one operator who walked through Spanish CCs because English was a second language, one scaled-account graduate who routes into the cohort. Discipline outcomes only. No income claims. 4. The Scorecard step (4:30-5:30) - "Before you buy LPP, take the 90-second readiness quiz. It tells you whether you are ready, too early, or ready for the cohort. The quiz is free." 5. Close (5:30-6:30) - "Take the quiz. If it routes you into LPP, the next step is on the page. If you are too early, go watch the daily live - free, every morning. If you are ready for the cohort, the application is on the cohort page."

Production: Ricky records to camera at the Techbuds Trading HQ. AG1 edits, captions, embeds. Spanish variant filmed same day.

Service-mapping: AG1 writes the script, directs the record, edits the final cut, embeds on both surfaces, sets the captions and the autoplay logic. We do not film without Ricky. The voice is Ricky's.

Piece Two. The Readiness Scorecard

Surface: /scorecard subdomain or /ready landing on learnplanprofit.net (final URL locked with Ricky day one).

Question structure (10-12 questions, 90-second flow): 1. Have you ever placed a real-money trade? (none / paper only / fewer than 10 real / 10-100 real / 100+ real) 2. Capital available to trade with right now (under $1K / $1-5K / $5-25K / $25-100K / $100K+) 3. Risk tolerance in plain language (cannot afford to lose anything / can afford small drawdown / can afford 10%+ drawdown without emotional change) 4. Time available for the 9:30 AM EST live (none / 1-2x per week / 3-4x per week / daily) 5. Prior trading-education courses taken (none / one / two-plus / LPP already) 6. Current trading PnL stage (have not started / down / break-even / up small / up scaled - all in bands, no exact numbers asked) 7. Language preference (English / Spanish / both) 8. Day-trading style (long / short / both / unsure) 9. Specific interest (nasdaq ETFs / blue-chip stocks / both / not sure yet) 10. Goal in next 90 days (learn the basics / build a repeatable plan / scale account size / unsure) 11. Self-rated readiness 1-10 (calibration vs the score the system returns) 12. Email + first name (capture)

Output: - 0-39 = "Too Early" - routed to the free YouTube playlist + a TOFU nurture sequence - 40-69 = "Ready For LPP" - routed to the VSL on learnplanprofit.net + the Teachable checkout - 70-100 = "Ready For The Cohort" - routed to the cohort landing + the application form

Plus a 3-line bottleneck readout per scorecard taker - the specific gap (capital, time, plan adherence, prior experience) that determined the route.

Tags applied to the email list: - score band (early / LPP-ready / cohort-ready) - capital band - language preference - daily-live engagement signal (set on the daily-live opt-in capture) - prior LPP graduate (yes / no)

Service-mapping: AG1 builds the scorecard logic, the scoring algorithm, the routing rules, the bottleneck readout templates, the email-tagging integration. We do not write the curriculum recommendations. Ricky writes the recommendations once - the scorecard runs forever.

Piece Three. The LPP Operator Cohort

Surface: /cohort or /operator landing on learnplanprofit.net (final URL locked with Ricky day one).

Format (proposed, locks with Ricky day one): - 6-8 verified day traders per cohort - 4 cohorts per year (quarterly cadence) - Price band: mid-four-figure to low-five-figure per operator (locked with Ricky day one) - 2-day in-person component at Techbuds Trading HQ in Arizona + 90-day implementation runway - Application-gated via Typeform uTtFirS4 - Bilingual seats welcome (English / Spanish in-room support available, leveraging the existing Spanish CC infrastructure)

Filter (in the application): - LPP graduate or equivalent trading-education completion - Currently trading live (not paper) - the gate is "you have made real trades with real money" - Capital position consistent with scaling (in bands, not exact) - Ready for portfolio-level risk discipline at the operator level - Willing to fly to Arizona for the 2-day in-person

Modules (cohort curriculum framework, locks with Ricky day one): - Module One - Plan Audit. Walk every operator's current plan in the live group format. Surface the plan gaps. Lock the revised plan before any trade size changes. - Module Two - Risk Discipline At Scale. Position sizing, max-loss-per-day rules, max-loss-per-week rules, the journaling cadence. Not a tactic class, an operator-discipline class. - Module Three - The 9:30 AM EST Live, From The Trader Side. The 2-day in-person Arizona block. Trade alongside Ricky from the Techbuds Trading HQ. Live debrief at end of each session. - Module Four - The 90-Day Runway. Cohort group call cadence post-in-person. Weekly journal review. The graduation criteria (plan adherence over a 60-day window, not P&L).

Service-mapping: AG1 builds the cohort landing page, the application Typeform, the scorecard-to-cohort routing, the post-application email sequence, the in-person logistics email surface, the cohort group communication surface (Discord channel or Skool space), the post-cohort alumni email cadence. We do not run the cohort. Ricky runs the cohort.

Piece Four. The Daily Live Capture

Surface: /watchlist (or /lista for Spanish) landing on learnplanprofit.net + a pre-live opt-in widget embedded on the YouTube live URL via channel description and pinned-comment routing.

Mechanism: - Pre-live (the night before): a simple landing "Get tomorrow's watchlist before the 9:30 AM open" captures email - Pre-live email (5:30 AM EST): the watchlist of nasdaq ETFs and blue-chip names Ricky is watching for the open. No specific trade recommendations. Levels, ranges, prior-day-close references. Plus the link to the daily live. - Post-live email (11:30 AM EST or end-of-day): 3-bullet recap. The discipline log (what plan Ricky followed, not what he made). Tomorrow's pre-open prep window. The next LPP / scorecard / cohort touchpoint depending on the recipient's tag band.

Retargeting pixel: - Fires on the daily-live YouTube URL via the opt-in surface - Builds the warmest possible audience for any future paid-media spend (Year-2 lever, not Year-1)

Service-mapping: AG1 builds the watchlist landing, the daily recap email template (Ricky writes the watchlist itself, AG1 builds the system), the pre-live and post-live cadence automation, the retargeting pixel wiring, the language segmentation (English / Spanish). We do not write the watchlist. The analyst voice on the watchlist is Ricky's.
